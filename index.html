How I got started

Growing up in sunny Miami, my career began in the real estate field, allowing me to meet people from all walks of life and greatly develop my communications and sales skills. However, art and design were always my inner passion, and when a close friend told me about web development, I decided to take a look and was instantly captivated!
I started my journey as a self-taught developer in 2018 using resources such as Code Academy and various YouTube tutorials, and although these tools gave me a great jump-start into the field, I felt that I needed a more robust program to solidify my proficiency. After much research I decided to take a front-end certificate course at Noble Desktop located in New York City, and upon completion I knew that I was ready for the next step.
What fascinates me about web development and coding in general is the constant process of learning, building, creating and ultimately delivering a beautiful and functional product to the end user.
